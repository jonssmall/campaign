About Anthony Colucci

Long term resident of Toms River, Anthony Colucci has dedicated over 15 years of his life to serving our community. As an over two decade resident of Ward 1, he understands the unique challenges and opportunities our neighborhood faces.

Anthony's commitment to public service has been a throughline in his entire career when he volunteered his time with local organizations and community development initiatives. His hands-on approach and ability to bring people together have made him a trusted voice in our community.

With a background in finance, community organizing and local government, Anthony has the experience and vision needed to tackle Ward 1's most pressing issues, from infrastructure improvements to economic development and public safety.

"I'm running because I believe our community deserves leadership that listens, acts, and delivers results for every family in Ward 1."
